PAT[I/O]
A Weekly Public Rehearsal Platform

PAT[I/O] is a weekly public rehearsal platform, where PAT students can share their creative work and collaborate with their peers.

Fridays 5-7pm in Davis

PAT students can book up to one hour of time, indicate if it's open for collaboration, and use any resources available in Davis*.

Department of Performing Arts Technology

School of Music, Theatre & Dance

University of Michigan

*Davis training is required for participation.
